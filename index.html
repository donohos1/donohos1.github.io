Project description

The goal of this project is to assist our clients at ThinkPath by performing a feasibility study
examining and assessing the merits and viability of available
industry offered Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solutions, leveraging Open
Source or Off-the-Shelf (OTS) resources, intended as a replacement for their existing
ThinkPRO application.

Our goal as a team for Capstone II, is to grow our knowledge base and improve our development
skills while assisting our clients at ThinkPath. We thank our sponsors for giving us the
opportunity to improve our skills and learn about the inner workings of a real world company.
